## Welcome to Zephyra

Zephyra is a modular backend platform designed for modern decentralized systems and enterprise automation.  
Our services are built with **Node.js + TypeScript + Firebase**, supporting **authentication**, **KYC**, **contract management**, and **activity tracking**.
